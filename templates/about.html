About Kindora

Kindora is a heartfelt initiative dedicated to supporting and empowering orphaned children. Founded by a passionate team – Shivamani Yadav, Akshay Kumar,mounika, Indra, Bhavana, and Raghu Yadav – our mission is to create a safe, nurturing environment where every child can thrive.

Through our platform, we connect generous donors with children in need, ensuring transparency and trust in every contribution. At Kindora, we believe that even small acts of kindness can make a big difference, and together, we can give these children hope, education, and a brighter future.

Join us in making a lasting impact and spreading compassion, one child at a time.
